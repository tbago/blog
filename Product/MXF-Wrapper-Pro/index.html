MXF Wrapper Pro

2020-04-21 | Product

MXF Wrapper Pro is a simple and powerful video tools.It is designed for camcorder and rewrap mxf files to mov format.

Supported output formats:

  • Final Cut Pro(X) prefered video formats:Apple Prores 422,Apple Prores 422 HQ,Apple Prores 422 LT, Apple Prores 422 Proxy, Apple Prores 4444(This codec is depended on FCP and ProApps QuickTime Codecs,If you do not install,this format will be hidden).
  • Final Cut Express / iMovie format: Apple Intermediate Codec(AIC) (This codec is depended on FCP and ProApps QuickTime Codecs,If you do not install,this format will be hidden)
  • Rewrap mxf file direct to mov format.This is compatible with FCPX,Quick Time and iMovie.

Key features:

  • Support all mxf format like op-1a(Sony camcorder or Canon camcorder),op-atom(Pansonic camcorder) and so on.
  • Support for all popular editing formats(ProRes,DNxHD,etc) and non-linear editors(Final Cut Pro,Avid Media Composer, Adobe Premiere Pro, Apple iMovie)
  • Support multi audio stream,all audio stream will be rewrap into the output file.
  • Faster than transcoding
  • No generation loss
  • Preserves metadata like TimeCode
  • Automatically detect P2 card struct,you can directly import the MXF video and extra audio will be automatically detected.
  • Support add folder directly.

Rewrap or Transcode
By working with camera manufacturers to seamlessly parse metadata, MXF Wrapper Pro is able to quickly convert MXF file into playable QuickTime files,without generation loss or transcoding.In addition, MXF Wrapper Pro support for transcoding to all of the popular post production format.
